Welcome to Moneta! We are here to help you start saving for your future in a simple but effective way. If you are looking for mind-numbing, complicated investment advice, then we have no idea how you found us. That’s not what we do. The goal of this site is to walk you through the process of opening a Roth IRA and teach you the basics of investing without boring you to death.

There are four main parts to this process that we will help you with:

  • Choosing an investment bank
  • Learning some basic fundamentals of investing
  • Helping you identify what kind of investor you are
  • Choosing the right kind of investments based on your needs

One important thing to know before we get started: Moneta is an educational site. We will give you the information you need to make your own smart decisions. As you move you through the steps, you will directed to the website of the investment bank that you choose. We suggest keeping Moneta open, so you can easily move back and forth between as needed.

Now, let’s get rolling!
